Lines

by: Drake Haehnichen

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I wanted to make something other than an adventure game or platformer. I also wanted it to be a skill based game.

2. What I Learned

Before the testing the gameplay was just the same with no changes, after user testing the game speeds up as time goes on, and there's a hard mode

3. Architecture of My Game

The main class is where all of the calculations and other things happen and the other two are for the visuals.

4. Technical Implementation

The vertical distance between the lines is decided through a random number being generated for a vertical distance between lines, and another random number being generated that decides how many lines in a row will either go up or down. There's also a feature that prevents the lines from going off screen and instead reseting by smoothly going back into the middle. There's also another method that does the math to get the dimensions of the area were two connecting lines overlap.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging part was writing comments to know what things did. I just ended up writing what each variable does, and what each section in a certain method does.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

Rather than just coming from the right to left, I've thought about having the direction the lines come from and the direction they go be constantly rotation. I also would like to make the game look better.
